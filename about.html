About MUSE

ELEGANCE. EXECUTION. ELEVATION.

Our Mission

With a passion for celebration, MUSE seeks to elevate our best moments by enhancing the drinking experience with premium RTD cocktails and beverage catering services. We want to provide beverages that won't take patrons away from the party.

With a focus on flavor and client relations, it's our desire for all of our clients to celebrate amongst the clouds — as if each cause for celebration will be etched in the stars of eternity.

What We Stand For

Three pillars that guide every drop we craft.

01

Flavor First

Every MUSE recipe starts and ends with flavor. We iterate relentlessly until each cocktail earns a personality of its own — one that stands out in a crowded RTD market.

02

Celebrate Ceaselessly

MUSE was created to elevate the moments worth remembering. It's not about making memories — it's about living the legends we'll tell for years to come.

03

Empower Inspiration

Built from the audacity to pursue passion, MUSE creates a platform where the silent and meaningful individuals who inspire us may be celebrated and inspire others.
