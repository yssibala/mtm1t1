01 · PRIVACY POLICY

How we handle your information

MUSE (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this site you agree to the practices described here.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly — your date of birth for age verification, items added to your cart, and the name, email, shipping address and message you submit through our contact, checkout or “Share Your Muse” forms. We also collect limited technical data automatically, such as device type, browser, and pages visited, through cookies and similar technologies.

HOW WE USE IT

We use your information to verify legal drinking age, process and fulfill orders, respond to inquiries, improve our products and site, and — where you have consented — to send marketing communications and personalize content. We do not sell your personal information.

SHARING & RETENTION

We share information only with service providers who help us operate the site and fulfill orders (payment processors, shipping carriers, age-verification and analytics vendors), and where required by law. We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by applicable law.

YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to opt out of marketing or the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. To exercise any right, contact us. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

02 · COOKIE POLICY

Cookies & your choices

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We use strictly necessary cookies to run the site (remembering your cart and age confirmation), analytics cookies to understand how the site is used, and personalization cookies to tailor content. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be switched off.

When you first visit, our consent banner lets you accept all cookies or decline non-essential ones. You can change your choice at any time by clearing this site’s data in your browser, which will prompt the banner again. For more detail on the categories we use, contact us.

04 · SHIPPING & RETURNS

Delivery & returns

Shipping. We ship only to states and jurisdictions where the direct-to-consumer sale and shipment of alcohol is permitted. An adult 21+ with valid government ID must be present to sign for delivery; we cannot leave alcohol unattended. Orders typically ship within 2–4 business days.

Returns. Due to laws governing alcohol, we cannot accept returns of opened product. If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 30 days and we will make it right with a replacement or refund where permitted.
